    Hand Gesture Recognition System
    Dec 2019-Apr 2020

    Project is based on hand gesture recognition which becomes a challenging task in computer vision field especially after the appearance of mobile devices. Although many algorithms are available ,it is difficult to meet real time situations and robustness in different lightening conditions and backgrounds. Our project improves hand gesture algorithms algorithms for computationally limited handheld android devices. We used HOG,FWNP features on the enhanced NUS dataset. HOG is a feature used in computer vision for object detection. FWNP is computed on the image for obtaining local neighborhood information of pixels.

    Detecting diabetic retinopathy using deep learning
    Jun 2020-July 2021

    Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is caused because of diabetes, which mainly damages the retinal part of the human eye and thereby impact vision. If it isn’t recognized early, it can prompt visual deficiency. Since, DR is irreversible, so surgery only results in inaccurate vision. But the risk of vision loss can be substantially minimized by earlier recognition and surgery of DR. Generally, an ophthalmologist needs more time, effort, and expensive, for manual detection of fundus image of DR retina and at last, it may lead to misdiagnosis, which could be perfectly analyzed by computer-based diagnosis systems. So, in today’s digital world deep learning is one of the popular techniques which yields better results for processing and classifying the medicalized images and also in many more sectors. Hence in our system for processing of patient's DR retina fundus image we used a deep learning method which, mainly consists of convolutional neural networks and can be highly effective.
